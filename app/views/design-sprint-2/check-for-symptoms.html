The only way to know if you have chlamydia is to get a test. You can get a test even if you have no symptoms.

Do you have any of these symptoms?

  • discomfort or pain when urinating (weeing)
  • unusual vaginal discharge
  • bleeding between periods (even if you use hormonal contraception like the pill or an implant)
  • bleeding during or after sex
  • pain during sex
  • pelvic pain (in women)
  • any discharge from your penis or bottom
  • pain in your testicles
