Samples will be collected from the areas you have symptoms using one or both of the following methods:

  • swab - a small cotton bud which is wiped over the area with symptoms
  • urine (wee) collection

The samples will be sent to a lab for testing.

If you see a sexual health expert, they'll ask questions about your symptoms and sexual activity to understand what kind of test you need.

What would you like to do?

You can see your GP. They might refer you to a sexual health expert. You could be asked to pay a prescription charge if you need treatment.
