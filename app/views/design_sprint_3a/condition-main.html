About chlamydia

Chlamydia is the most common sexually transmitted infection (STI). Men and women can get it.

Anyone who has unprotected sex can get chlamydia.

You could have chlamydia even if you don't have any symptoms, or your symptoms go away.

The only way to know if you have it is to get a chlamydia test.

It's easy to treat with medical help.
