Where to order a self-test kit online

RU Clear
Website: www.ruclear.co.uk
Covers: Greater Manchester Authority
You can usually order a free self-test kit if you're under 25 years old. If you can't get a free kit, you can still buy a kit. You'll have to send your samples by post to be tested.
