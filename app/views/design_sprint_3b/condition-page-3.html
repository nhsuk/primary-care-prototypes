Who should get tested for chlamydia

If you have a new sexual partner, you should both get tested for chlamydia.

You and your sexual partner (or partners) should get tested if you or they:

  • have symptoms
  • have unprotected sex with someone else - check which contraception protects you from chlamydia
  • have a sexually transmitted infection (STI)
  • had sex with someone who has chlamydia or a different STI
