Treatment

If you have chlamydia a health professional will give you antibiotics. It's usually one of the following:

  • azithromycin - you'll take 2 or 4 tablets at once, by mouth
  • doxycycline - you'll take 2 capsules a day for 1 week, by mouth

You might be given antibiotics before you get your test results. This could happen if it's likely that you have chlamydia, for example you have symptoms.

After you've finished the antibiotics, you may be told to have another chlamydia test to check it's gone.

Retesting

After 10 days after treatment, get a chlamydia test.
