Protect yourself from chlamydia

To protect yourself from getting or passing on chlamydia, you should:

  • use a male or female condom every time you have vaginal or anal sex
  • cover the penis with a condom, or the vagina or bottom with a dental dam (latex or soft plastic square) when you have oral sex
  • use a dental dam if you are a female and you rub your vagina against a female partner's vagina
  • avoid sharing sex toys - if you do share them, you should wash them or cover them with a new condom before anyone else uses them
