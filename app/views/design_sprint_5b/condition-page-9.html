If chlamydia is not treated

If you have chlamydia and don't treat it, it can spread to other parts of the body and lead to serious health problems. These are known as complications.

The more times you have chlamydia, the more likely you are to develop complications.

Complications in females

  • pelvic inflammatory disease
  • ectopic pregnancy
  • infertility
  • inflammation and pain in the liver

Complications in males

  • infection in the testicles
  • low fertility
