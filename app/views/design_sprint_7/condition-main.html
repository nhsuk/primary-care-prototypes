About chlamydia

Chlamydia is one of the most common sexually transmitted infections (STIs). Anyone who has oral, vaginal or anal sex can get it or pass it on.

You could have chlamydia even if you have no symptoms, or your symptoms go away.

Chlamydia is easy to treat with medical help.
