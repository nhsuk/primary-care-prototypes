What happens during the test

You or a sexual health professional will take samples from the areas that might be infected. This can be done by:

  • wiping a cotton bud inside the vagina, bottom or mouth
  • peeing into a container

Your samples will be sent to a lab and tested for chlamydia. You'll usually get your results by phone or text message within 2 weeks.

If you see a health professional they'll also ask questions about your sexual activity and symptoms. This will help them decide what samples to take.
