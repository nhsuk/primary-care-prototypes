How to get tested for chlamydia

You can get tested for chlamydia by:

  • going to a sexual health clinic
  • getting a home test kit - you can order one online or pick one up from somewhere nearby
  • seeing your GP

Many sexual health clinics offer a walk-in service, where you don't need an appointment. They'll often get test results quicker than GP practices.

Get tested for chlamydia

There are different ways to get tested, depending on your age and location. You may be able to get a kit to test yourself.

Find a chlamydia test

What happens during the test

You or a health professional will take samples from areas that might be infected.

This can be done by:

  • wiping a cotton bud inside your vagina, anus or mouth
  • peeing into a container

Your samples will be sent to a lab and tested for chlamydia. You'll usually get your results by phone or text message within 2 weeks.
