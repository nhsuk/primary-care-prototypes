How to get tested for chlamydia

You can get tested for chlamydia by:

  • going to a sexual health clinic
  • getting a home test kit - you can order these online or pick them up from places like pharmacies
  • seeing your GP

Many sexual health clinics offer a walk-in service, where you don't need an appointment. They often get test results quicker than GP practices.

What happens during the test

You or a health professional will take samples from areas that might be infected.

This can be done by:

  • wiping a cotton bud inside your vagina, anus or mouth
  • peeing into a container

Your samples will be sent to a lab and tested for chlamydia. You'll usually get your results by phone or text message within 2 weeks.
