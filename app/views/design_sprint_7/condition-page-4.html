How you get chlamydia

Chlamydia is a bacteria you can catch by:

  • having unprotected vaginal, anal or oral sex
  • sharing sex toys - if you don't wash them or cover them with a new condom

It can also be passed from a pregnant woman to her baby.

When you won't catch chlamydia

You can't get chlamydia from:

  • kissing or hugging
  • swimming pools, toilet seats, sharing baths or towels
  • cups, plates or cutlery
