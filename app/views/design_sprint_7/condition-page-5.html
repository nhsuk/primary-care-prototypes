Treatment for chlamydia

You’ll be given antibiotics if you have chlamydia. You can only get these from a health professional.

You’ll usually be given either:

  • 2 or 4 tablets you take on one day
  • 2 capsules you take once a day for 1 week

Sometimes you might be given antibiotics before you get your test results if it's likely you have chlamydia.

When you can have sex again

Don’t have vaginal, oral or anal sex (even with a condom or oral sex dam) until you and your sexual partner have completed treatment.

If you had the one-day course of antibiotics, don’t have sex for at least 1 week.

This is so you don't pass on the infection or get it again.

When to get tested again

You won’t usually need to go back for another test after you’ve been treated.

But sometimes a health professional may recommend getting tested again after a few weeks or months. This is to check the infection has gone and you haven’t become infected again.

If you're under 25 and sexually active, it’s recommended you keep getting tested every year and whenever you change sexual partner.
