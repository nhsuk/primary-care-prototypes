How to prevent chlamydia

To avoid getting or passing on chlamydia and other sexually transmitted infections (STIs):

  • use a condom every time you have vaginal or anal sex
  • cover the penis with a condom, or the vagina or anus with an oral sex dam (latex or soft plastic square) when you have oral sex
  • use an oral sex dam if you rub your vagina against a partner's vagina
  • avoid sharing sex toys - if you do share them, wash them or cover them with a new condom before anyone else uses them
