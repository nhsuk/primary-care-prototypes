Telling partners you have chlamydia

If you have chlamydia, you should tell any recent sexual partners that they could also have it.

They should get tested for chlamydia.

If you don't want to do this yourself, a health professional can do it for you. This is called partner notification.

They won't notify anyone unless you ask them to, and you’re able to provide contact details for the people you've had sex with.

They won’t give your name to anyone they notify.
