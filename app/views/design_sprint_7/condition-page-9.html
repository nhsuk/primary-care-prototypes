If chlamydia isn’t treated

If you don’t get treatment, chlamydia can spread to other parts of your body and lead to serious health problems. These are known as complications.

The more times you have chlamydia, the more likely you are to get complications.

Complications of chlamydia include:

  • infection of the womb, fallopian tubes and ovaries (pelvic inflammatory disease) – this can cause problems like infertility
  • infection of the testicles
  • joint pain
  • inflammation and pain around the liver
